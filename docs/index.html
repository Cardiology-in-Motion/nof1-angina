N-of-1 Angina

Angina Symptom Diary

An "N-of-1" approach focuses on one person — you. Track your symptoms over time, spot patterns, and share meaningful reports with your healthcare team.

Features

📕

Symptom Diary

Log chest pain episodes with severity, duration, location, and triggers. Quick-log option for fast entries on the go.

📋

Questionnaires

CCS Angina Grading to track your quality of life and symptom severity over time.

🏃

Dundee Step Test

Monitor your exercise tolerance with a guided step test built right into the app.

📈

Charts & Trends

Visualise symptom patterns and questionnaire scores over time to understand your progress.

📄

PDF Reports

Generate professional reports summarising your data. Share directly with your cardiologist via email or AirDrop.

💊

Medications

Keep a simple record of your current medications, always to hand at appointments.

Your data stays yours

Your data syncs via iCloud to your other devices. No data is shared with third parties. No accounts, no tracking.

This app is for personal symptom tracking only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your healthcare professional.
