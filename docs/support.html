Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers or the cloud.

How do I share a report with my doctor?

Go to the Report tab, tap "Generate PDF Report", then use the share sheet to send it via email, AirDrop, or any other method.

How do I delete a diary entry?

In the Symptom Diary tab, swipe left on any entry and tap "Delete".

What happens to my data if I uninstall the app?

All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app. Consider generating a PDF report before uninstalling if you want to keep a record.
